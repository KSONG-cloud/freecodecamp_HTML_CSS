Classic Cheesecake Recipe

This is a classic recipe for cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Heavy Cream

Instructions

  1. Cream the cream cheese with sugar.
  2. Add in eggs one at a time.
  3. Add in heavy cream and vanilla.
  4. Pour into baking tin and bake at 160C for 1 hour.
