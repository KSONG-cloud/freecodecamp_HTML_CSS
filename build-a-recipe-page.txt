    <h1>Classic Cheesecake Recipe</h1>
    <p>
      This is a classic recipe for cheesecake.   
    </p>
    <img src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/labs/recipe.jpg" alt="Three eggs and a whisk.">
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Cream cheese</li>
      <li>Egg</li>
      <li>Sugar</li>
      <li>Vanilla</li>
      <li>Heavy Cream</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Cream the cream cheese with sugar.</li>
      <li>Add in eggs one at a time.</li>
      <li>Add in heavy cream and vanilla.</li>
      <li>Pour into baking tin and bake at 160C for 1 hour.</li>
    </ol>
